Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is Mexican food made of mashed avocado and served with tortilla chips

Ingredients

  • 4 Ripe avocados
  • 2 Juiced limes
  • 1 Garlic cloves
  • 1/4 Teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Add the lime juice, garlic, and salt to a medium-sized bowl. Stir to combine
  2. Cut the avocados in half, removing the pits
  3. Scoop the flesh into the bowl with the lime juice, garlic, and salt
  4. Mash the avocado with a fork until a creamy dip forms, leaving some large avocado chunks for texture
  5. Serve immediately as a dip or topping
